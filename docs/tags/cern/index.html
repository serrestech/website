Software development at CERN

in meetups

December 11, 2018

Get the official Poster This presentation will provide an introduction to CERN and its operations, focusing on software development and the various opportunities available. Afterward there will be a dive into the development process which is followed on the applications, presenting the various technologies, frameworks and practices followed. Moreover we will dive into the Accelerators Logging Service one of the major big data projects at CERN and will demonstrate data analysis with Spark on Hadoop (Jupyter).
