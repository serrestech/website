Our Members

We are open to any people. Have a look what our members say about SerresTech.

  • SerresTech is one of the most interesting technology communities I have ever met.
    Friendly and open, it is a place to share new ideas, meet new people and watch interesting talks.
    For me it was a big surprise that a small city in Greece - Serres - has such an active technology related community.

    Theodoros Ploumis

    Web developer

  • SerresTech and the team behind it, is a ‘tech oasis’ for our little town and university. The events they create are unique, with a lot of love, passion and knowledge. I wish them luck and many more events! Thanks guys!

    Anestis Zioulis

    Undergraduate Student

  • The diversity of people, culture, and knowledge are my favorite parts of the SerresTech community. The speakers do not only work in Greece but come from different countries all around the world. Apart from the presentations, the community organizes career days and Hackathon competitions which are helpful and fun. The SerresTech community is the best home for every junior and senior IT professional who wants to expand his knowledge and meet new people.

    Pavlina Mitsou

    Undergraduate student

  • SerresTech is a big surprise for such a small city as Serres. With a lot of meetups covering tons of topics in a junior friendly manner which helps learning. Additionally there is a yearly event called hackathon in which the members form teams and compete, making the whole community a whole more interactive!!

    Michael Michailidis

    Software Engineer

  • Friendly community with captivating meetups that would motivate anyone to learn new technologies and methodologies. Interesting, enthusiastic and innovative crowd, a very enjoyable social group for any level of technical experience. Personally I had the opportunity to meet new people, expose myself to new ideas and concepts and discuss with like-minded developers about intuitive architectures and practices.
